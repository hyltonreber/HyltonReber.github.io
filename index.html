About Me

I graduated from the University of Idaho in May 2025 with a B.S. in Mechanical Engineering and an Aerospace Engineering Academic Certificate. My coursework spanned thermodynamics, fluid mechanics, fatigue & fracture mechanics, gas dynamics, and turbomachinery.

My capstone project with Hyster-Yale had me building a physics-based modal testing rig — programming Arduino accelerometers in C++, executing a 38-node test procedure, and running FFT analysis in MATLAB to isolate resonance frequencies in an overhead guard.

Outside the lab, I co-founded Axes & Axles LLC to compete in the Red Bull Soapbox Race Denver 2026 — designing and fabricating a full vehicle from the ground up and engineering a field replacement brake system 24 hours before competition.
